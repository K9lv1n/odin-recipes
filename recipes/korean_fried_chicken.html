Korean Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Dredge chicken in flour and shake off excess.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F.
  4. Fry chicken for 10-12 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
